Overview

Basketball is a key event at the Olympics, showcasing top talents from around the world. The competition features both men's and women's tournaments, each with a group stage followed by knockout rounds.

The group stage involves round-robin matches, with the top teams advancing to the knockout stage, culminating in the quarterfinals, semifinals, and medal matches.

Results

Men's Basketball Tournament

  • Gold Medal: USA

    The USA Men's Basketball team continued their dominance by winning the gold medal, defeating France 90-85 in the final.

  • Silver Medal: France

    France put up a strong fight, especially on their home ground, but ultimately fell short in the final.

  • Bronze Medal: Australia

    Australia secured the bronze medal with a convincing win over Slovenia, 107-93.

Women's Basketball Tournament

  • Gold Medal: USA

    The USA Women's Basketball team also continued their winning streak, defeating China 82-68 in the final to clinch the gold.

  • Silver Medal: China

    China made an impressive run to the final, showcasing their growing strength in women's basketball.

  • Bronze Medal: Australia

    Australia's women's team earned the bronze medal by defeating France 74-70 in a closely contested match.
