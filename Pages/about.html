About Paris and Asian Games

Paris Games

The Paris Games have a rich history dating back to the early 20th century. Known for their cultural impact, the games have been a symbol of unity and sportsmanship.

Paris is set to host the upcoming Summer Olympics in 2024, promising a blend of traditional French elegance and modern innovation.

Asian Games

The Asian Games, also known as Asiad, is a multi-sport event held every four years among athletes from all over Asia. It is the second-largest multi-sport event after the Olympics.

The first Asian Games were held in 1951 in New Delhi, India. Since then, the event has grown significantly in scale and importance.
