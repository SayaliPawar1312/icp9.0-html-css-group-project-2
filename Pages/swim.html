Swimming Overview at Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Details

  • Event Dates: July 27 - August 9, 2024
  • Venue: La DÃ©fense Arena, Paris

Overview

Swimming is one of the most anticipated events at the Olympics, featuring a wide range of races across different styles and distances. The competition includes the following events:

  • Men's and Women's Freestyle (50m, 100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, 1500m)
  • Men's and Women's Backstroke (100m, 200m)
  • Men's and Women's Breaststroke (100m, 200m)
  • Men's and Women's Butterfly (100m, 200m)
  • Men's and Women's Individual Medley (200m, 400m)
  • Men's, Women's, and Mixed Relays (4x100m Freestyle, 4x200m Freestyle, 4x100m Medley)

Swimming is known for its intense competition, with athletes from around the world aiming to break records and win medals. The USA, Australia, and China are among the top contenders in these events.

Key Events and Results

Men's 100m Freestyle

  • Gold: Caeleb Dressel (USA)
  • Silver: Kyle Chalmers (Australia)
  • Bronze: Kliment Kolesnikov (Russia)

Women's 200m Butterfly

  • Gold: Zhang Yufei (China)
  • Silver: Regan Smith (USA)
  • Bronze: Hali Flickinger (USA)

Mixed 4x100m Medley Relay

  • Gold: USA Team
  • Silver: China Team
  • Bronze: Australia Team
